9.1 Find mail server providers:-
Step 1 :- Go to https://www.misk.com/tools/#dns. Then enter your domain name e.g. gmail.com. Press
enter and see the details.
Look for the MX record and observe the name to the right of it, which is the domain’s email provider
e.g.(mail.yaxmail.net) . You can contact this company or one listed as the nameservers in regards to the
domain’s email.

Step 2 IP whois:-
Copy the IP address and perform WHOIS search to find Company providing service for that IP address.
